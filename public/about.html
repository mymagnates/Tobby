About

Magnates Solution LLC • Plano, TX

Magnates Solution LLC is a software and operations-focused company based in Plano, TX, built by people with hands-on property ownership and management experience.

Over more than 12 years of real-world home ownership and property management work, we experienced the same challenges many owners and managers face every day: fragmented records, manual follow-ups, delayed communication, and too many disconnected tools.

Handout was created to solve those operational gaps. Our goal is to help property owners and property managers run smarter, cleaner workflows with one practical system that supports day-to-day execution and long-term visibility.

The platform is designed to organize property data, standardize recurring processes, support better communication, and connect service providers when help is needed. We focus on useful, reliable features that reduce friction and improve decision quality.

Our operating principles are straightforward: Ease, Clarity, Intelligence, Connection, and Action.
